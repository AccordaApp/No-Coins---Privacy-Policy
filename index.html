Privacy Policy for No Coins

Last updated: August 22, 2025

Simple Summary: No Coins does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. Your privacy is completely protected.

Information We Collect

None. No Coins does not collect any personal information, usage data, or any other information from users.

Information We Store

None. No Coins does not store any user data on our servers or any third-party services. All game data (chip counts, player information) is stored locally on your device only.

Information We Share

None. Since we don't collect any information, we have nothing to share with third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

No Coins does not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks that would collect user information.

Local Data

Any game settings or preferences you configure within the app are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Since No Coins does not collect any information from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data collection practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the app or app store updates.

Third-Party Services

No Coins does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect user data.
